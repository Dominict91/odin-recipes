Baconcheesefries

Best Baconcheesefries the north

lovely home made Baconcheesefries by dominic taylor

Ingredients

  • 1 package (32 ounces) frozen French fried potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • Ranch salad dressing
    • /

Recipe instructions (taken from my head )

  1. Cook French fries according to package directions./li>
  2. Place fries on a broiler-proof dish or platter.
  3. tSprinkle with cheese, onions and bacon
  4. Boil for 1-2 minutes or until cheese is melted
  5. Serve with ranch dressing.
